Privacy Policy

Inboxr Pro protects operator data like classified intel. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

Data We Collect

  • Identity and contact details for authorized stakeholders.
  • Operational telemetry (delivery metrics, agent sessions, campaign health).
  • Voice, email, SMS, and social conversation logs tied to your workflows.

How We Use Data

  • Provision and maintain your private Titan Node infrastructure.
  • Monitor deliverability, agent uptime, and campaign performance.
  • Improve AI agent quality and security posture with anonymized telemetry.

Security Commitments

  • Dedicated infrastructure with isolated access controls.
  • Encryption in transit and at rest for sensitive datasets.
  • Continuous monitoring for abuse and policy violations.

Your Rights

You can request exports of your data at any time. You control campaign assets, lead lists, and messaging logs. On termination, your data is securely wiped within 24 hours.
